Shopify Partners

Shopify Partners Program: The Shopify Partners Program is a free program designed for freelancers, developers, designers, agencies, and marketers who build on Shopify. By joining, partners can create development stores, build apps, design themes, and refer merchants — earning revenue shares and exclusive benefits. Partners get access to powerful tools, early feature previews, documentation, and support to grow their businesses while helping merchants succeed on Shopify.

Shopify Engineering

Shopify Engineering: Shopify Engineering refers to the technical team and community that build and maintain Shopify's platform. They focus on scaling infrastructure, improving performance, developing APIs, creating apps, and introducing new e-commerce technologies. Shopify Engineers share innovations and challenges publicly through the Shopify Engineering Blog, offering deep technical insights into systems like Hydrogen (for headless commerce), Shopify Payments, and global storefront expansion.

Shopify Community

Shopify Community: The Shopify Community is an open, supportive forum where merchants, developers, designers, and experts discuss Shopify-related topics. Users can ask questions, share best practices, find troubleshooting advice, and learn about new features. It’s a go-to place for networking, getting feedback, and staying updated with announcements from Shopify. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, the community encourages collaborative learning and problem-solving.
